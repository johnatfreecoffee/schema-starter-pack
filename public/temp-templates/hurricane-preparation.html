Hurricane Preparation Frequently Asked Questions

When should I start hurricane preparation?
Begin hurricane preparation in April-May for best availability and pricing. This allows time for comprehensive inspections, material ordering, and installation before peak season. Early preparation also gives you peace of mind and ensures you're not scrambling when storms approach.
What's included in hurricane preparation services?
Our services include roof inspection and reinforcement, storm shutter installation, structural fortification, drainage optimization, emergency supply preparation, and insurance documentation. We customize each package based on your property's specific vulnerabilities and your budget.
How much does hurricane preparation cost?
Costs range from $899 for essential protection to $4,999 for maximum defense packages. The final cost depends on your home's size, current condition, and specific needs. We provide detailed estimates after our initial assessment, and many improvements qualify for insurance discounts.
Does insurance cover hurricane preparation costs?
Many insurance companies offer discounts or partial coverage for hurricane preparation work. We work with all major carriers and can help with documentation. Some improvements like impact windows and storm shutters may qualify for significant premium reductions that offset the installation cost over time.
Can I do hurricane preparation myself?
While some basic preparation tasks can be DIY projects, professional services ensure code compliance and proper installation. Professional work includes structural reinforcement, shutter installation (especially upper floors), roof repairs and upgrades, drainage system improvements, and building code compliance work. We recommend professional assessment even if you plan to do some work yourself.
How long does hurricane preparation take?
Timeline depends on the scope of work. Essential inspections and minor repairs take 1-2 days. Complete preparation with shutters and reinforcement typically takes 3-5 days. Maximum defense packages may require 1-2 weeks. We work efficiently to complete preparation before storm threats, and emergency services are available during active season.
