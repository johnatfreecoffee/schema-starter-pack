PRESSURE WASHING QUESTIONS ANSWERED

Pressure Washing Questions Answered

How often should I have my property pressure washed? +
Most homes benefit from annual cleaning, though specific surfaces may need more or less frequent attention. Roofs typically need cleaning every 2-3 years, while driveways may need annual or bi-annual cleaning depending on use and staining.
Is pressure washing safe for all surfaces? +
Not all surfaces can handle high-pressure cleaning. That's why we assess each surface and use appropriate methods soft washing for delicate materials and pressure washing for durable surfaces. Professional assessment prevents damage.
What's the difference between pressure washing and soft washing? +
Pressure washing uses high-pressure water to physically remove dirt and stains from durable surfaces. Soft washing uses low pressure with specialized cleaning solutions to chemically break down and remove organic growth and stains from delicate surfaces.
Will pressure washing damage my landscaping? +
Professional pressure washing includes protecting your landscaping through pre-treatment watering, covering sensitive plants, and using eco-friendly cleaning solutions. We take extensive precautions to prevent any landscape damage.
How long does professional pressure washing take? +
Timing varies based on property size and services selected. A typical house exterior cleaning takes 2-4 hours, while comprehensive property cleaning may take a full day. We provide accurate time estimates during your free consultation.
Can pressure washing remove all stains? +
Most stains can be removed or significantly lightened with professional cleaning. Some permanent staining (like rust on concrete) may not fully remove but will be dramatically improved. We'll assess stain types during inspection and set realistic expectations.
