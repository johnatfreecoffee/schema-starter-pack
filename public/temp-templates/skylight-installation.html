Frequently Asked Questions About Skylight Installation

How long does skylight installation take?

Most residential skylight installations are completed in 1-2 days, depending on the complexity and number of units. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your home.

Will a skylight cause leaks?

When properly installed by professionals like Clear Home, skylights are completely weatherproof. We use proven flashing techniques and high-quality materials to ensure leak-free performance.

Can skylights be installed in any room?

Most rooms can accommodate skylights, including bathrooms, kitchens, living areas, and bedrooms. Our experts assess your roof structure and recommend the best solutions.

What's the maintenance requirement?

Skylights require minimal maintenance. We recommend annual inspection and cleaning. Venting skylights may need occasional lubrication of moving parts.

How much does skylight installation cost?

Costs vary based on skylight type, size, and installation complexity. Contact us for a free consultation and detailed quote for your specific project.

Are skylights energy efficient?

Modern skylights with Low-E glass and proper installation can actually improve home energy efficiency by reducing artificial lighting needs and providing natural ventilation.
