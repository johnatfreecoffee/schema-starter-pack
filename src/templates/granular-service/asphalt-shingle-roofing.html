Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about asphalt shingle roofing in {{city_name}}

How long does an asphalt shingle roof last?

With proper installation and maintenance, asphalt shingle roofs typically last 20-30 years. Premium architectural shingles can last even longer. Factors affecting lifespan include climate, ventilation, maintenance, and quality of installation. Regular inspections and prompt repairs help maximize your roof's longevity. {{company_name}} uses only high-quality materials and expert installation techniques to ensure your roof reaches its full potential lifespan.

What's the difference between 3-tab and architectural shingles?

Three-tab shingles are thinner, lighter, and more affordable with a flat, uniform appearance. Architectural shingles (also called dimensional or laminated shingles) are thicker, more durable, and have a layered, textured look that adds depth and visual interest. Architectural shingles typically last longer, offer better wind resistance, and come with longer warranty periods. Our team can help you choose the best option for your home's style and budget.

How much does a new asphalt shingle roof cost?

Asphalt shingle roofing costs vary based on roof size, pitch, complexity, shingle quality, and removal requirements. Typical costs range from $5,000 to $15,000+ for average-sized homes. We provide free, detailed estimates that account for all factors specific to your project. {{company_name}} offers transparent pricing with no hidden fees, and we work with various financing options to fit your budget. Contact us at {{company_phone}} for a personalized quote.

Can I install new shingles over my existing roof?

While it's sometimes possible to install new shingles over one existing layer, we generally recommend complete tear-off and replacement. This allows us to inspect the roof deck for damage, ensure proper ventilation, and provide a better foundation for your new roof. Layering shingles can void manufacturer warranties, add excessive weight, and hide underlying problems. During your free inspection, we'll assess your specific situation and recommend the best approach for long-term performance.

How long does asphalt shingle roof installation take?

Most asphalt shingle roof replacements take 1-3 days for average-sized homes, depending on roof size, complexity, weather conditions, and whether tear-off is required. We work efficiently while maintaining high quality standards, and our experienced crews minimize disruption to your daily routine. We'll provide a specific timeline during your consultation and keep you informed throughout the project. Rush services are available for emergency situations.

What warranty comes with asphalt shingle roofing?

Your new asphalt shingle roof includes two warranties: manufacturer's material warranty (typically 20-50 years depending on shingle type) and our workmanship warranty covering installation. {{company_name}} provides lifetime workmanship warranty on all installations, and we help register your manufacturer warranty to ensure full coverage. Yes! {{company_name}} has extensive experience working with insurance companies on storm damage claims. We provide detailed documentation, photos, and professional assessments to support your claim. Our team can meet with insurance adjusters, explain the scope of damage, and ensure you receive fair coverage. We handle the technical aspects so you can focus on getting your home restored.
