## TRANSACTIONAL COPY FOUNDATION

**Before writing ANY copy, understand:** We target customers ready to take action NOW, not browsers seeking information. Every word moves them toward calling or booking.

**Transactional Intent Signals to Weave Throughout:**

- Immediate need language: "today," "now," "fast," "emergency," "same-day"
- Action-ready phrases: "schedule your," "get started," "book your," "request your"
- Problem-solution framing: Lead with pain point, immediately offer solution
- Location + Service combinations: "{{service_name}} in {{city_name}}" throughout
- Decision-stage language: "ready to," "it's time to," "don't wait," "before it gets worse"

**Why Transactional Copy Matters:**

- Broad keywords like "residential roofing" attract browsers
- Specific phrases like "emergency roof repair {{city_name}}" attract buyers
- Every page should answer: "Why should I call {{business_name}} RIGHT NOW?"

### 2. BODY COPY

**Transactional Copy Structure (Use This Pattern):**

```
[Problem/Need] + [Solution] + [Why Us] + [Action]

Example: "Dealing with storm damage in {{city_name}}? {{business_name}} provides
fast, reliable restoration with {{years_experience}}+ years of local experience.
Our licensed team responds within 24 hours. Call {{phone}} for your free inspection."
```

**Opening Sentence Rules:**

- First sentence: Acknowledge their problem/need immediately
- Second sentence: Position {{business_name}} as the solution
- Third sentence: Provide proof ({{years_experience}}, credentials)
- Final sentence: Clear next step/CTA

**Urgency Triggers to Include:**

- "Before minor damage becomes major repairs"
- "Don't let water damage spread to your home's interior"
- "Limited availability during storm season"
- "Insurance claim deadlines approaching"

- Use {{service_name}} + {{city_name}} combinations
- Reference: "Your {{city_name}} neighbors trust us"
- Include surrounding service areas when relevant
- Add local landmarks or area references where natural

**Additional Requirements:**

- Complete copy for each content block
- Match brand voice from context
- Use {{business_name}} for ALL company references
